Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 03, 2025

Welcome to Hoppenings! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Information: With your consent, we collect your location to show you nearby breweries and calculate distances.

Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data to improve our app, including:

  • Screen views and navigation patterns
  • Time spent on different features
  • App interactions (map usage, filters, etc.)
  • Device information (platform, app version)

Brewery and Event Data: We display brewery and event information but do not collect personal data about these interactions.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide location-based brewery recommendations
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share anonymous, aggregated data with analytics providers.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Instagram Data Usage

Hoppenings connects to the Instagram Graph API to retrieve publicly available content (captions, media URLs, and timestamps) from participating brewery business accounts. This data is used solely for displaying brewery events and beer releases within the Hoppenings app. No Instagram user credentials, private messages, or personal information are collected or stored.

Your Rights (Colorado Privacy Act)

Colorado residents have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your personal data
  • Data portability
  • Opt-out of data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@hoppenings.com

Analytics and Tracking

We use Firebase Analytics to improve features. Data is anonymous and does not identify individual users. You can opt out via device settings or by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services:

  • Google Maps (location/directions)
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Supabase (data storage and management)

Please review their privacy policies for more information.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents can contact us if they believe their child has submitted data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in-app and with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at hoppeningsco@gmail.com

Colorado residents may also contact the Colorado Attorney General's Office if they have concerns about our practices.
